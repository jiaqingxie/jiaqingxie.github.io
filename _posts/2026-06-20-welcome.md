This is the first post on my new blog. I plan to write here about my research in
**AI for physical science**, graph neural networks, generative models, and the
occasional thought on academic life.

## Why a blog?

Papers are polished and slow. A blog lets me share rough ideas, lessons learned,
and notes that might be useful to others working in similar areas.

## What to expect

- Short notes on papers I find interesting
- Reflections on my research projects
- Practical tips on tooling and reproducibility

Thanks for reading — more soon!
