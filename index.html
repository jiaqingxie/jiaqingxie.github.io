About Me

Hi, I'm Jiaqing! I am a research assistant at the Shanghai AI Lab AI for Science Center. I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science from ETH Zurich in 2025, and my B.Eng. in Electronics and Computer Science from the University of Edinburgh in 2022. I will start my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Fudan University in September 2026.

My research focuses on AI for physical sciences, generative models (flow matching and diffusion models), graph neural networks and graph transformers, and their applications to chemistry, spectroscopy, and molecular modeling. You can see my papers on Google Scholar or download my CV.

If you want to reach me, email me at godxie.steven@gmail.com.
