Jiaqing Xie

I'm a Researcher at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (SAIL), supervised by Dr. Yuqiang Li and Prof. Tianfan Fu. My Research interests include Artificial Intelligence for Chemistry and Materials, Graph Representation Learning, and Machine Learning Optimiaztion. Before coming to SAIL, I got my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Electronics at Edinburgh University (Rank Top 3), and my master's degree in Computer Science at ETH Zurich (5.4 / 6.0, Thesis 6.0).
