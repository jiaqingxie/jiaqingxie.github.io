Hi! I'm a MSc CS student @ ETH Zurich. My broad research interests lie in Theoretical Machine Learning, which include:

  • Graph Neural Networks expressiveness, robustness and theories of graph neural networks, learning graph representation on natural sciences (GraphAI4Science).
  • Optimization and Learning Theory explorations on traditional machine learning algorithms, gradient descent variants on convex/non-convex/convex-convace optimization problems.
  • Robotics and SLAM An investigation of GNN and learning theory into robotics and slam field, such as feature matching, PGO with GNN.
