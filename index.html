About

My research sits at the intersection of machine learning, chemistry, and scientific reasoning. I am interested in building models, benchmarks, and systems that help AI interact more naturally with molecular structure, spectra, and research workflows.

Current themes include spectroscopy as a machine learning interface, structure-aware molecular foundation models, and agentic systems for chemistry.
