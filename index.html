About Me

Hi, I'm Jiaqing! I am a first-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Fudan University. I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science from ETH Zurich in 2025, and my B.Eng. in Electronics and Computer Science from the University of Edinburgh in 2022.
