Brief Bio

Jiaqing Xie is a PhD student in Computer Science at Fudan University. His research interests include spectral methods, graph theory, approximation theory, and AI for Science.

He received his M.Sc. in Computer Science from ETH Zurich, where he followed the Theory of Computing Systems (TCS) track. Before that, he received his B.Eng. degree from the University of Edinburgh.
